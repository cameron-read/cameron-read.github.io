what is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand, use, and manage your own emotions in positive ways to relieve stress, communicate effectively, empathise with others, overcome challenges and defuse conflict to name a few.

How is it different from IQ?

IQ or intelligence quotient differs from EQ or emotional quotient in that it pertains to an individual's technical prowess, cognitive ability and general knowledge of things such as word comprehension, math skills, abstract and spatial thinking.

Why is EQ important?

Because it affects so many facets of an individual's life, both personally and professionally. directly affecting how one deals with complex and negative emotion, to how an individual creates, develops and nurtures relationships. Combining in the ability for an individual to regulate their own emotional state ina positive way and therefore being more mindful of others around them, being able facilitate the others in lowering their negative emotion and stress as well as their own.
