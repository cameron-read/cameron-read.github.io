neuroplasticity..."what is it to me..."

Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity which is the science behind what we now know about the ever growing and malleable nature of the mind, helps oneself to grasp the concepts that our minds are forever growing, changing and getting better. Intelligence for example is not a predetermined fixed state of mind, but something that with practice can be nurtured and encouraged, the mind with its adaptable nature can like any muscle in the body can become stronger, new skills can be learnt, even mastered at any stage in one's life. Although it is something that I knew of personally the principles of neuroplasticity was not something I would think of often, in fact I had somewhat resigned to the fact that I was too old to learn anything new.

growth mindset a better state of mind...

In shaking off the stigma of failure and the lack of belief in my own capabilities, what I have come to know as having a “fixed mindset”. I believe now after extensive research into neuroplasticity and “growth mindset” that this is in fact not the case. My mind is capable of far more than I gave it credit for. Knowing these facts regarding my mind helps to keep me focused and at the very least assured that even though I may struggle at times during this journey I will prevail so long as I remain committed to the path and trust in my amazing ever evolving plastic brain… to paraphrase Carol Dweck I will endeavour to embrace the potential yet, and shake off the tyranny of now.
